Frequently Asked Questions

What is tracQR?

A website making your metro journeys more accessible by means of online payment and qr code generation as well as features including min. price vs min. interchange.

How does it calculate the fare?

The fare depends on the distance, the time, and the amount of stops.

What is the time limit of issuance of the ticket?

1. For mobile ticket:- One hour issuance before the journey

2. For offline ticket:- One hour before issuance before the journey

Can I extend my journey beyond the original destination stop?

Yes, the fare would be deducted when your ticket is scanned at the metro station you scan it on.
